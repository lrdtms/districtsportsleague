About District Sport League

District Sport League (Pty) Ltd (DSL) is a women-led, impact-driven company specializing in corporate team building, communication development, wellness initiatives, and sport-based training programmes.

We exist to help individuals and organisations grow stronger through experiential learning, using sport and team-based methodologies to unlock better communication, collaboration, and performance. At the same time, we are deeply committed to female athlete empowerment, particularly within the South African netball ecosystem.

Who We Are

Founded in Stellenbosch, District Sport League is 51% Black-owned and 100% women-led, with a strong focus on transformation, inclusivity, and sustainable development.

Our work bridges two worlds:

  • Corporate performance and workplace wellbeing
  • Athlete development and community upliftment

By combining these, we create meaningful impact both on and off the court.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower people and organisations through innovative, sport-inspired learning experiences that:

  • Strengthen teamwork and communication
  • Improve wellbeing and morale
  • Develop leadership and performance
  • Create pathways for female athletes to thrive professionally

What We Do

We offer a range of tailored services designed to meet the needs of corporate, educational, and non-profit organisations:

  • Corporate team-building workshops (indoor & outdoor)
  • Communication and collaboration training
  • Staff wellness and sports-based engagement activities
  • Leadership and performance development programmes
  • Fundraising and social impact events

Each programme is designed with intention, professionalism, and measurable outcomes in mind.

Our Impact

Beyond corporate delivery, DSL is committed to long-term social and economic impact. Our initiatives focus on:

  • Creating sustainable employment opportunities
  • Supporting emerging netball athletes in their transition from amateur to professional sport
  • Delivering Early Childhood Development (ECD) netball programmes
  • Providing mentorship, skills development, and leadership exposure to young women

Our Values

Everything we do is guided by four core values:

  • Empowerment – unlocking potential in people and organisations
  • Inclusivity – creating access and opportunity for all
  • Professionalism – delivering excellence at every level
  • Impact – focusing on meaningful, measurable outcomes

Leadership

District Sport League is led by Dr. Freda Kemp, Director, whose vision and leadership drive our commitment to transformation, excellence, and sustainable growth within both enterprise development and professional sport.
