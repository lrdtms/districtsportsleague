Dr. Freda Kemp Recognized for Leadership in Sports Development

Published: December 15, 2025

District Sport League Director receives accolades for her contribution to transformation and professional sport excellence. Dr. Freda Kemp's visionary leadership has earned widespread recognition from the sports development community and beyond.

A Career of Impact

Dr. Kemp's journey in sports development spans decades of dedication to creating opportunities for athletes, particularly women in netball. Her work has consistently focused on breaking down barriers and building sustainable pathways for athletes to thrive both on and off the court.

Recognition and Awards

Recently, Dr. Kemp was honored at the National Sports Development Awards for her outstanding contribution to transformation in South African sport. The recognition specifically highlighted her innovative approach to combining corporate development with athlete empowerment through District Sport League.

The awards panel noted: "Dr. Kemp has demonstrated exceptional leadership in creating sustainable models that benefit both corporate clients and aspiring athletes. Her work exemplifies the transformative power of sport in building stronger communities and organizations."

Transformation Through Innovation

What sets Dr. Kemp's approach apart is her unique ability to bridge different sectors. Under her leadership, District Sport League has developed programmes that serve dual purposes:

  • Delivering high-quality corporate team-building and wellness initiatives
  • Creating employment and development opportunities for female athletes
  • Building sustainable revenue streams that fund grassroots sports development
  • Establishing mentorship networks that support emerging talent

Leading by Example

As a woman leader in the sports industry, Dr. Kemp has become a role model for aspiring entrepreneurs and sports professionals. Her commitment to empowerment extends beyond her programmes to her leadership style, which prioritizes inclusivity, professional excellence, and measurable impact.

Vision for the Future

Despite the recognition, Dr. Kemp remains focused on the future. In her acceptance speech, she emphasized that the real measure of success is the number of lives transformed through access to sport and opportunity. She outlined an ambitious vision for District Sport League to expand its reach while deepening its impact in existing communities.

"This recognition belongs to every young athlete who has trusted us with their dreams, every corporate partner who has believed in our approach, and every team member who works tirelessly to make our vision a reality," Dr. Kemp stated. "We're just getting started."

Community Response

The announcement has been met with widespread celebration from the communities and organizations that District Sport League serves. Athletes, corporate partners, and community leaders have expressed their congratulations and shared stories of how Dr. Kemp's leadership has positively impacted their lives.
