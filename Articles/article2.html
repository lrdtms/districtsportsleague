Empowering Female Athletes: ECD Netball Programme Expands

Published: December 28, 2025

Our Early Childhood Development netball programme reaches new communities, bringing sport and mentorship to young athletes across South Africa. This expansion marks a significant milestone in our commitment to female athlete empowerment and grassroots sports development.

Growing Reach, Growing Impact

What started as a local initiative in Stellenbosch has now expanded to reach multiple communities across the Western Cape. Our ECD netball programme provides young girls with access to quality coaching, equipment, and mentorship opportunities that were previously unavailable in their areas.

More Than Just Sport

While netball skills are at the heart of the programme, our approach goes far beyond teaching the game. We focus on holistic development that includes:

  • Life Skills Training: Leadership, communication, and teamwork
  • Educational Support: Homework assistance and academic encouragement
  • Nutrition Education: Teaching healthy eating habits for optimal performance
  • Mentorship: Connecting young athletes with role models and professional players

Community Partnerships

The success of our expansion has been made possible through strong partnerships with local schools, community centers, and sports facilities. These partnerships ensure that our programme is accessible, sustainable, and culturally relevant to each community we serve.

Creating Pathways to Professional Sport

One of our key objectives is to create clear pathways for talented young athletes to progress from grassroots participation to competitive and professional netball. We've already seen several participants move into regional development squads, with some showing exceptional promise for future national team selection.

Empowerment Through Sport

Beyond the court, we've witnessed remarkable transformations in the young athletes who participate in our programme. They demonstrate increased confidence, improved academic performance, and stronger community connections. Many have become leaders in their schools and communities, inspiring other young girls to pursue their dreams.

Looking Ahead

As we continue to expand, our vision remains clear: to empower every young girl with the opportunity to discover her potential through sport. We're actively seeking additional partners and supporters to help us reach even more communities in the coming year.
